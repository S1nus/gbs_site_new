WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 26, 2018

2018

THE TIMES CENTER, NEW YORK
Main Site

On September 26, 2018, Consensus welcomed 15 presenting companies to the stage at the NY TimesCenter in Manhattan to tell their stories to an audience of equity investors, lenders and major retailers. Each of those presentations, as well as Consensus founder Michael O’Hara’s introductory remarks and the keynote interview with Qurate Group CEO, Mike George, are available to friends of Consensus on this website. Also available are short interviews about key products with 14 of the presenting companies and each of the 15 companies who exhibited their products at the event. We hope you enjoy these videos and encourage you to contact Consensus with any questions. In some instances, the videos have shadows of people walking in front of the camera. We apologize for this and will reposition our camera to avoid this issue next year.

Legal Notice

These videos are offered with the consent of the presenters and exhibitors. However, no consent has been given to further distribute videos without the consent of Consensus and the respective presenter or exhibitor.
